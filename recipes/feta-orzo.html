Feta and Bell Pepper Orzo

Description

Ingredients

  • 250g orzo
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes
  • 400ml water
  • 3 spring onions
  • 1-2 bell peppers
  • 1 courgette
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 block feta
  • Chilli flakes to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius.
  2. Dissolve stock cubes in boiling water.
  3. Finely dice bell peppers, chop spring onions, finely grate courgette and crush garlic cloves.
  4. Add orzo, peppers, spring onions, garlic, courgette and olive oil to an oven proof dish and mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour over stock, stir and add chilli flakes to taste.
  6. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring periodically.
  7. Cut feta into cubes.
  8. When orzo is almost cooked, remove the foil and add in your cubed feta.
  9. Bake for a further 10-15 minutes or until desired consistency is reached.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

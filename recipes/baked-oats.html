Baked Oats

Description

These TikTok Baked Oats are just like eating cake for breakfast. So easy to make with just a handful of healthy ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 medium-large ripe banana
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8-oz (250ml) capacity ramekin or spray with cooking spray.
  2. Place all ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Transfer to prepared ramekin, add your favorite toppings/mix-ins (see notes) and bake for 20 minutes, until puffed up and cooked through.
  4. Serve hot.

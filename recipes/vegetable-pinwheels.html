Vegetable Pinwheels

Description

Tortillas filled with cream cheese and veggies. Great for getting extra nutrition in on a busy day!

Ingredients

  • 1 flour tortilla
  • 50g reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1-2 tablespoons reduced-fat mayonnaise
  • 1/6 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1 spring onion
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • Broccoli florets to taste
  • Spicy cheddar to taste

Steps

  1. Grate carrot and finely chop bell pepper, broccoli and spring onion.
  2. Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise and dry seasonings.
  3. Add the vegetables to the cream cheese mixture and combine.
  4. Spread over tortilla.
  5. Sprinkle over a pinch of spicy cheddar to taste.
  6. Roll the wrap tightly and slice into 1/2 - 1 inch slices.
  7. Enjoy!
